The Swimulator is now partnered with SwimSwam magazine!

Welcome to the Swimulator! We have partnered with SwimSwam magazine to help provide additional exciting coverage of college swimming. Stay tuned for new content and more announcements in coming weeks.


Select a year, gender, and division and navigate to various swimming simulations using the top toolbar. Head to "conferences" if you wish to simulate conference and national swim meets. Go to "head to head" to simulate dual meets using past meet lineups and results or let the swimulator optimize your lineups. Top times gives the top times done during a given season. Placing calculates how well certain times would place in a given conference. Programs allows you to compare the strength of all swim programs.
